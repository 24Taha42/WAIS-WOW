The goal of this website is to ensure teachers and students have access to quality education. Quality education is the fourth UN Global Goal. The targets that will be focused on are Target 4.1 ("By 2030, ensure that all girls and boys complete free, equitable and quality primary and secondary education leading to relevant and effective learning outcomes"), Target 4.2 ("By 2030, ensure that all girls and boys have access to quality early childhood development, care and pre-primary education so that they are ready for primary education"), and Target 4.6 ("By 2030, ensure that all youth and a substantial proportion of adults, both men and women, achieve literacy and numeracy").
